Bamikole Olaleye Akinsehinde — Founder & CEO

PhD (In-View), MA, PGP-AI/ML, BSc, AFHEA, MNIM (Chartered)

With over a decade of experience across AI, data science, and software development, Bamikole leads Stepway Technologies in building technology that simplifies life and bridges people with practical solutions.

His journey began with Megastat Communication (2007) and FastLink Communications (2010), ventures that evolved into Stepway Technologies. He applies AI to complex challenges across finance, healthcare, agriculture, telecommunications, and energy sectors.

Currently pursuing a PhD in the UK alongside advanced AI/ML postgraduate studies, Bamikole combines technical expertise with research innovation. As an AFHEA and Chartered MNIM member, he champions responsible technology adoption and scalable solutions that address societal needs.
